The Cube

Start off by imagining that you are in a vast desert and in some area within what you can see from where you are there is a cube. It can be close to you, or you can imagine it's far away. Imagine what material it's made from. That can be water, metal, wood, or anything else you can imagine.

As soon as you have in mind where the cube is in relation to you, as well as what material the cube is made of, write that down. You can then move onto the next step.
