Interpreting the Cube

The cube is a representation of your ego or the way you view yourself. Its closeness to you can indicate how self-aware you are, or how much you recognize your own view of yourself. The material your cube is made of can also indicate certain things about you. A guide to what various materials mean in this context can be found on the next page.

Interpreting the Ladder

The ladder is a representation of your friends and family. Its closeness to you can indicate how close your relationships with those people are. The material your ladder is made of can also indicate certain things about your relationships with other people. A guide to what various materials mean in this context can be found on the next page.

Interpreting the Horse

The Horse is a representation of your romantic relationships. Its closeness to you can indicate how close your relationship with your significant other is (or potential significant other). Whether the horse is carrying anything can also indicate various things that are open to interpretation.

Interpreting the Storm

The storm is a representation of your anxieties. Its closeness to you can indicate how close you feel that your anxieties are (proximity in time). The storms intensity can indicate how troubling you feel that the problems in your life are.
