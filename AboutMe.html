About Me

My name is Daniel Musser. I grew up and currently live in Utah. I love working with computers in many different ways. I created this website called "The Cube Test" as a school project. I originally came up with the idea to do it because a friend of mine texted me one day and was trying to remember how to walk someone through a personality test, called "The Cube Test". I thought it would be helpful to have a place where everybody can go and learn it if they want.

Other Interests

Apart from coding, I really enjoy playing the Violin and Guitar in my free time. I play in a multinational band called "Phonagraph Blue". Professionally, I work as a banker at Bank of the West, which is a bank based out of California.
